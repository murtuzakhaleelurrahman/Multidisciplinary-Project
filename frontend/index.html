VIT Smart Transit: Next-Gen Campus Commuting

VIT Smart Transit is the premier bus tracking and fleet management platform designed specifically for campus mobility. Whether you are heading to the main gate, Katpadi Junction, or navigating through Vellore, our system ensures a seamless, predictable, and safe journey.

Why Choose Smart Transit?

📡

Live Bus Tracking

Track your bus in real-time with sub-second latency and plan your journey efficiently without waiting at the stop.

🚦

Traffic-Aware ETAs

Our smart routing engine analyzes segment traffic and historical data to give you highly accurate arrival times.

🚨

Crowdsourced Alerts

Help your fellow commuters by reporting delays or heavy traffic with our one-touch reporting system.

🛡️

Guardian Mode

Share a live tracking link with friends or family so they know exactly when you'll arrive at your destination.

How to Track Your Ride

  1. Search Routes: Enter your starting point and destination in the search bar above.
  2. Select Your Bus: View the live map to see available buses and their current occupancy levels.
  3. Monitor Arrival: Set an alert threshold to get notified exactly when your bus is approaching.
  4. Travel Safely: Board your bus and share your live status if needed.
